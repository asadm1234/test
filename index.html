Our Mission

We are TechBridge Georgia, a youth-driven nonprofit dedicated to bridging the digital divide in our communities. Founded by two passionate tech enthusiasts, we believe that access to technology shouldn't be a privilege—it should be a right. We combat digital inequality by refurbishing donated computers, providing them to underserved families, and reducing electronic waste. Our mission is simple: restore power to communities through technology, youth leadership, and sustainable solutions that create lasting impact.
